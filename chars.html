Gizmo

Gizmo the raccoon is the appointed leader of the Trash Buddies. He lives in the biggest oak tree in the center of the forest with his older brother, Squish. He prides himself in being a leader. He loves to provide for his neighbors and make everyone laugh. Though he tries to be fearless, he gets scared more than he lets on.

Rascal

Rascal the fearless possum doesn't have many worries. He lives in a Maple stump with his partner, Peaches. Rascal loves reading fantasy stories, as well as writing them. He loves baking with Peaches and playing with the other animals in the forest and Meadow.

Spud

Spud is a nervous little skunk. He lives in a den in the meadow with his best friend, Dumpling. His best friend uses to be Tycoon, until a random day when he turned mean. Spud's strengths are his brain and big heart. He has a crush on his pal, Mochi. Spud can spray when he gets nervous, but doesn't have the best control over the ability. His favorite food is Delectable Trash Porridge.

Peaches

Peaches the possum loves crafting. She is always creating clothes to keep her and her friends warm, as well as crafts to gift people she loves. She loves her partner, Rascal. Her best friend is Mochi, and they hang out multiple times a week.

Mochi

Mochi is a pretty skunk. She lives in a Maple tree with her new friend, Juniper. Mochi is very feminine and always wears flowers on her head. She loves hanging out with Peaches and the Trash Buddies. Her favorite food is Spud's Delectable Trash Porridge.

Squish

Squish is Gizmo's older brother. He loves to eat as much as he can. The extra food keeps him warm in the winter. Gizmo's closest friend is Dumpling. He loves his little brother but likes to pick on him. Squish loves to be a comic relief in hard times. He sometimes goes to the town with the Trash Buddies to help them.

Dumpling

Dumpling is a small spotted skunk. He is nervous about leaving his habitat, so he doesn't normally venture last the meadow and forest. His best friends are Spud and Squish.

Fang

Fang is the leader of thr Troublemakers. He is mean raggedy raccoon. He was always a bully, and he used to throw Squish's lunch on the ground when they were in school. He loves stealing other creatures' food. He leads the Troublemakers by making Tycoon and Corky fear him. He lives in the bins in the city, where he can always steal food from restaurants and prank humans.

Tycoon

Tycoon is a small raccoon. He used to live in the prairie and was best friends with Spud. Now he follows Fang, and he lives in the city. There is more to Tycoon than meets the eye, and the Trash Buddies will find that out soon.

Corky

Corky is a tiny rat with lots of vengeance. He grew up in the city with hundreds of brothers, but he recently started following Fang. He has a torn ear and is always dirty.

Rebel

Rebel is a beagle. He lives in a yellow house at the end of a cul-de-sac with his two moms. He loves playing with the Trash Buddies. He was adopted by his moms a couple years ago after living in the streets with his brother Renaldo, who he still sees occasionally. He is almost always happy.

Fawn

Fawn is a young adult deer who is in love with her sweetheart, Primrose. Fawn loves to take care of Rose and is very protective and loving. Fawn was affected by the bullying her and Primorse endured due to their relationship where they grew up, so they moved to the meadow a few years ago. They live by some fir trees and bushes that are next to a big flower field.

Primrose

Primrose is feminine and carefree. She loves flowers and butterflies. She loves playing with Clove and her other meadow friends. She loves Fawn more than anything.

Clove

Clove is a sweet brown bunny who loves playing with Cypress, Ponchy, and Sinnamon. She lives in a den by a weeping willow tree. She loves the idea of romance.

Cypress

Cypress is a little mouse. He lives in a burrow in the meadow with his colony. He admires Rascal and loves jumping up to give his friends hugs. He is small but mighty. He loves oats, corn, seeds, and some occasional cheese.

Ponchy

Ponchy is a young squirrel who lives in walnut trees by Rascal and Peaches with his best friend Sinnamon. He loves to run, jump, and play all day. He enjoys harvesting food and eating fruit.

Sinnamon

Sinnamon is a young female squirrel who is best friends with Ponchy. She is rambunctious and always moving. She is kind and always shares her food.

Rowan

Rowan is an older fox, the elder of the meadow. He lives in a den near Spud and Dumpling. He has a red wagon he lets the Trash Buddies borrow. He used to venture into town when he was younger, so he often has tips for the Buddies.

Lucida

Lucida is an older fox who lives in a log in the forest. She has a grumpy exterior until you get to know her, then you'll see she is kind, funny, and wise. She is always bickering with Rowan. She loves hot dogs.

Juniper

Juniper is a sweet young hedgehog who lives in a walnut tree with Mochi. She is new to the forest. She has social anxiety and doesn't get as involved with her community as she would like. She has a kind heart and an interest in art.
